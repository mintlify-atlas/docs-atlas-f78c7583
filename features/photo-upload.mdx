## Upload your photo

Getting started with Simpsonize Me! begins with uploading your photo. The process is designed to be simple and intuitive, so you can quickly transform your favorite images into authentic Simpsons-style portraits.

### Supported file formats

You can upload photos in the following formats:

- **JPG** - Standard JPEG image format
- **PNG** - Portable Network Graphics with transparency support
- **WebP** - Modern web-optimized image format
- **HEIC** - High Efficiency Image Format (commonly used by iPhones)

<Info>
The better the quality of your input photo, the better your AI yellow portrait will turn out.
</Info>

### File size limits

Your uploaded photo must be **up to 15MB** in size. This generous limit ensures you can upload high-quality photos without compression.

### Upload methods

You have two convenient ways to upload your photo:

<Accordion title="Drag & drop">
Simply drag your photo file from your computer and drop it into the upload area. This is the fastest way to get started.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Browse to select">
Click the upload button to open your file browser and select the photo you want to transform.
</Accordion>

### What photos work best

Our AI Simpsonizer handles any type of photo:

- **Selfies** - Perfect for creating your personal Simpsonized avatar
- **Group shots** - Transform your entire friend group or team
- **Family portraits** - Immortalize your family in Springfield style
- **Pet photos** - Even your furry friends can join the yellow cartoon universe

<Tip>
For best results, use photos with clear visibility of facial features and good lighting.
</Tip>

## Next steps

Once you've uploaded your photo, our advanced AI Simpsonizer will analyze your features and begin the transformation process. You'll receive 5 unique Simpsonized variations delivered straight to your email within minutes.
