## Quality factors

### Input photo quality

The quality of your Simpsonized portrait starts with your input photo:

<Accordion title="Photo clarity">
Clear, well-focused photos allow the AI to better analyze your features. Blurry or out-of-focus images may result in less detailed portraits.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Lighting conditions">
Well-lit photos help the AI identify facial features accurately. Avoid photos with harsh shadows or extreme backlighting.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Face visibility">
Make sure faces are clearly visible and not obscured by hands, objects, or other people. The more the AI can see, the better it can transform.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Image resolution">
Higher resolution photos provide more detail for the AI to work with, resulting in more refined Simpsonized portraits.
</Accordion>
