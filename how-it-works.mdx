# How it works

Getting your photo transformed into Simpsons style with Simpsonize Me! is a breeze. Our streamlined process takes just minutes from start to finish.

## The 3-step process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload">
    ### Upload your photo
    
    Simply drag and drop your favorite photo or browse to select it from your device.
    
    **Supported file formats:**
    - JPG
    - PNG
    - WebP
    - HEIC
    
    **Maximum file size:** 15MB
    
    <Tip>
    The better the input, the better your AI yellow portrait. Use clear, well-lit photos where facial features are clearly visible for the best results.
    </Tip>
    
    **What works best:**
    - Selfies with good lighting
    - Professional headshots
    - Group photos
    - Family portraits
    - Pet photos
    
    Our AI Simpsonizer handles it all, whether you're uploading a single person or a group shot.
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI Magic">
    ### Our AI gets to work
    
    Once you've uploaded your photo, our advanced AI Simpsons generator springs into action:
    
    **What happens during processing:**
    1. **Feature analysis** - The AI analyzes your unique facial features, expressions, and characteristics
    2. **Style transformation** - Your photo is transformed into the authentic Simpsons yellow cartoon style
    3. **Variation generation** - Five completely unique portrait variations are created
    4. **Quality optimization** - Each portrait is optimized to professional, high-resolution quality
    
    <Info>
    Our AI technology meticulously captures not only your likeness but also your unique personality and quirks, making every Simpsonized image a true masterpiece.
    </Info>
    
    **Processing time:** Just minutes! Our lightning-fast AI ensures you don't have to wait long to see yourself in Springfield style.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download">
    ### Receive and download your portraits
    
    Within minutes of uploading, you'll receive an email with a link to download your Simpsonized creations.
    
    **What you receive:**
    - **5 unique Simpsonized variations** - Multiple options to choose your favorite or use them all
    - **High-resolution digital images** - Perfect for printing, sharing on social media, or creating custom merchandise
    - **Instant digital delivery** - Straight to your email, no waiting
    
    <Tip>
    Save all five variations! Each one captures different aspects of your personality and gives you options for different uses.
    </Tip>
    
    **How to download:**
    1. Check your email inbox for the delivery email
    2. Click the download link provided
    3. Access your personal download page
    4. Download all 5 high-resolution portrait variations
    
    Your files are ready to use immediately - share them on social media, print them as gifts, or use them as unique avatars!
  </Step>
</Steps>
