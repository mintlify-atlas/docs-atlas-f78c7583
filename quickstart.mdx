# Quickstart guide

Get your first Simpsonized portrait in just a few minutes! Follow this guide to transform your photo into an authentic Simpsons-style cartoon.

## What you'll need

Before you start, make sure you have:

- A photo you want to Simpsonize (selfie, family photo, pet photo, or group shot)
- Supported file format: JPG, PNG, WebP, or HEIC
- File size up to 15MB
- Payment method for secure checkout via Stripe

<Tip>
For best results, use a clear photo with good lighting and visible facial features. See our [photo tips guide](/guides/photo-tips) for detailed recommendations.
</Tip>

## Get Simpsonized in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare your photo">
    Choose a photo that shows clear facial features and good lighting. The better your input photo, the better your Simpsonized results will be.
    
    **Supported formats:**
    - JPG
    - PNG
    - WebP
    - HEIC
    
    **Maximum file size:** 15MB
    
    <Note>
    Works with any type of photo: individuals, couples, groups, families, and even pets!
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload and checkout">
    1. Visit [Simpsonize Me!](https://simpsonizeme.co/)
    2. Drag & drop your photo or click to browse and select it
    3. Complete the secure checkout process using Stripe
    4. Submit your order
    
    <Info>
    Your payment is processed securely through Stripe. Join over 15,000+ satisfied customers who trust our service!
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download your portraits">
    Within minutes, you'll receive an email with a download link containing:
    
    - **5 unique Simpsons-style portrait variations**
    - **High-resolution digital images**
    - Ready for printing, sharing, or creating custom merchandise
    
    Download your portraits and start using them right away!
    
    <Tip>
    Save all 5 variations - each one captures different aspects of your personality and style!
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
